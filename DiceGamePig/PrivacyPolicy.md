# **【小豬骰子 - 隱私權政策】**【中文版】


發布日期：2025 年 11 月 12 日
&nbsp;
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

### 引言

《**小豬骰子**》（以下簡稱「本應用程式」或「我們」）深知隱私權對您的重要性，並致力於保護您的個人資料。本政策旨在說明當您使用我們的行動遊戲應用程式時，我們如何處理資料。

**請注意：本應用程式是單機遊戲，不要求登入，不使用我們的伺服器儲存任何使用者自製的對話或個人資料。**



### 一、我們收集的資訊類型及處理方式

由於本應用程式是純單機遊戲，我們致力於最小化數據收集。

**我們不會收集您的姓名、電子郵件地址、電話號碼等直接的個人識別資訊（PII）。**

您的自製遊戲對話內容只會**本地儲存於您的設備內**，我們和任何第三方服務均無法存取這些內容。

我們僅會透過以下分類，自動收集**非個人識別**的技術和使用資訊，用於 App 的運營和改進：

| **資料類型** | **收集目的** | **資訊說明** |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **技術與設備資訊** | 確保 App 兼容性、提供廣告服務和防範欺詐。 | 包括您的 IP 地址、設備型號、作業系統版本、時區和語言設定。 |
| **應用程式使用數據** | 追蹤 App 的性能、功能使用頻率、遊戲進度，以優化遊戲體驗和調整遊戲參數。 | 這些數據不與您的個人身份相關聯。 |
| **廣告數據** | 在您同意的情況下，用於展示個人化廣告及衡量廣告活動效果。若您拒絕，則僅顯示非個人化廣告。 | 廣告識別碼及廣告互動記錄。 |
| **交易數據** | 處理和驗證遊戲內購交易。 | 購買內容、購買時間。 |



### 二、資訊的處理目的與法律依據 (針對全球用戶)

我們依照以下法律依據來處理您的數據：

| **處理目的** | **處理依據** |
|:--- |:--- |
| **提供和優化服務** (運行遊戲、錯誤修復、遠端配置) | **履行合約所需** / **我們的合法利益** (確保App穩定運行) |
| **提供廣告服務** (顯示和衡量廣告) | **您的同意** (個人化廣告) / **合法利益** (非個人化廣告) |
| **處理交易** (驗證內購) | **履行合約所需** (處理您的購買請求) |
| **安全與合規** (防欺詐、應對法律請求) | **我們的合法利益** / **法律義務** |



### 三、資料共享與揭露

您的非個人識別數據會與以下第三方服務共享，用於其提供的服務範圍內：

- **廣告服務：** Google AdMob
- **分析與配置服務：** Google Firebase (包括 Analytics 和 Remote Config)
- **支付處理：** Apple App Store

我們與這些第三方服務共享數據的目的僅限於實現功能。我們不會出售您的任何數據。此外，我們可能會在符合法律規定、司法機關或行政機關的要求下，揭露您的資訊。



### 四、全球數據傳輸聲明

本應用程式提供給全球使用者。我們處理您的資訊可能涉及將您的數據傳輸至您所在國家/地區以外的司法管轄區（例如美國），該地的數據保護法可能與您所在國家/地區不同。

透過使用本應用程式，您同意此類數據傳輸。我們承諾將採取所有必要的步驟，確保您的數據依照本隱私政策及適用法律得到安全處理（例如，透過實施標準合約條款 Standard Contractual Clauses, SCCs）。



### 五、兒童隱私權保護承諾

本應用程式分類為**適合所有年齡層的骰子遊戲**。

我們不會在知情的情況下，收集任何可直接識別個人的資訊（PII），包括未滿 13 歲兒童的 PII。所有收集的數據均為**匿名或假名化**的技術數據。

我們已配置我們的廣告服務（AdMob）和分析服務（Firebase）以**禁用個人化廣告**和**限制數據收集**，確保不收集或使用 13 歲（或適用地區的法定年齡）以下兒童的個人識別資訊，以嚴格遵守所有適用的兒童隱私法規（如 COPPA 和 GDPR-K）。



### 六、您的權利與選擇 (GDPR / CCPA)

作為使用者，您對於自己的個人資料享有以下權利：

- **拒絕追蹤 (ATT)：** 您可以在 iOS 設備的「設定」中管理本 App 的追蹤許可，以控制是否接收個人化廣告。
- **存取與更正權：** 您有權要求存取、更正我們持有的關於您的技術數據。
- **刪除權（被遺忘權）：** 您有權要求我們刪除我們收集的關於您的技術數據。
- **限制處理權：** 在某些情況下，您有權限制我們處理您的數據。
- **反對權：** 您有權反對我們基於「合法利益」處理您的數據。
- **CCPA/CPRA 聲明：** 我們不會出售或分享您依據 CCPA/CPRA 所定義的個人資訊。


### 七、資料安全與在地儲存

- **本地儲存：** 您的所有自製遊戲對話內容**只儲存於您的 iOS 設備本地**。如果您刪除 App，這些本地儲存的內容也會被移除，我們無法復原。
- **數據安全：** 我們採取合理的技術與組織安全措施來保護收集到的技術資訊。



### 八、政策變更與聯繫方式

本政策可能會不時修訂。建議您定期檢視本政策。

如果您對本政策有任何疑問、意見或疑慮，或希望行使您的任何權利，請透過以下方式與我們聯繫：

- **開發者名稱：** LadyRK (CHIH WEI CHANG)
- **電子郵件地址：** ladyrk.kcrk@gmail.com

# **【DiceGamePig - Privacy Policy】**【English Version】

&nbsp;

### Introduction

**"DiceGamePig"** (referred to as "we" , "us" or the “App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle information when you use our mobile game application.

**Please Note:** This App is a single-player, offline-first game. It does not require user login, and we do not use our servers to store any user-created content (dialogue) or personal data.



### I. Information We Collect and Process

We are dedicated to minimizing data collection. **We do not collect any Direct Personal Identifiable Information (PII)** such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Your custom game dialogue content is **stored locally on your device only**. Neither we nor any third-party services can access this content.

We automatically collect only the following categories of **non-personal technical and usage information** through third-party services for the sole purpose of operating and improving the App:

| **Data Type** | **Purpose of Collection** | **Description** |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **Technical & Device Information** | To ensure App compatibility, deliver advertising services, and prevent fraud. | Includes your IP address, device model, operating system version, time zone, and language settings. |
| **Application Usage Data** | To track App performance, feature usage frequency, and game progress (e.g., scores, crash reports) for optimization and configuration. | This data is not linked to your personal identity. |
| **Advertising Data** | Used, with your consent, to show personalized ads and measure ad effectiveness. If you decline tracking, only non-personalized ads are shown. | Advertising Identifier (e.g., IDFA) and ad interaction records. |
| **Transaction Data** | To process and validate in-app purchases. | Details of items purchased and time of purchase. |



### II. Legal Basis for Processing (For Global Users)

For our global users, particularly those in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

| **Purpose of Processing** | **Legal Basis (GDPR/Equivalent Laws)** |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Service Provision & Optimization** (Running the App, bug fixes, remote config) | **Necessity for Contract Performance** / **Our Legitimate Interests** (Ensuring App stability and functionality) |
| **Advertising** (Displaying and measuring ads) | **Your Consent** (For personalized ads) / **Legitimate Interests** (For non-personalized ads) |
| **Transaction Processing** (Validating purchases) | **Necessity for Contract Performance** (Fulfilling your purchase request) |
| **Security & Compliance** (Fraud prevention, legal requests) | **Our Legitimate Interests** / **Legal Obligation** |



### III. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your non-personal technical data is shared with the following third-party services necessary for the App's function. They process data according to their own privacy policies:

- **Advertising Services:** Google AdMob
- **Analytics & Configuration Services:** Google Firebase (including Analytics and Remote Config)
- **Payment Processing:** Apple App Store

We will not sell or rent your data. We may also disclose information when required to do so by law, court order, or governmental request.



### IV. Global Data Transfer Statement

This App is offered to global users. Processing your information may involve transferring your data to jurisdictions outside your country of residence (such as the United States), where data protection laws may differ.

By using the App, you consent to such data transfer. We take all reasonable steps to ensure your data is treated securely, including using standard contractual clauses (SCCs) where required by law.



### V. Children's Privacy Commitment (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Since this App is categorized as a **dice game suitable for all ages**, we strictly adhere to all applicable children's privacy regulations (such as the US COPPA and EU GDPR-K).

We have configured our advertising (AdMob) and analytics (Firebase) services to **disable personalized advertising** and **limit data collection** for all users, particularly those under the age of 13 (or the relevant legal age), ensuring no PII is knowingly collected from children.



### VI. Your Rights and Choices (GDPR / CCPA)

As a user, you have rights regarding your data:

- **Right to Opt-Out of Tracking (ATT):** You can control whether the App can track your activity across other apps and websites via your iOS device's "Settings" menu.
- **Right of Access & Deletion:** You have the right to request access to or deletion of the technical data we hold about you.
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain conditions.
- **Right to Object:** You have the right to object to the processing of your data where the legal basis is our "legitimate interests."
- **CCPA/CPRA Statement:** We do not "Sell" or "Share" Personal Information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.



### VII. Data Security and Local Storage

- **Local Storage:** All your custom game dialogue content is **stored only on your local iOS device**. We cannot retrieve this content. If you delete the App, this local content will also be removed.
- **Data Security:** We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect the technical information we collect.



### VIII. Changes to This Policy and Contact Information

We may update this policy periodically. We encourage you to review it regularly.

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

- **Developer Name:** LadyRK (CHIH WEI CHANG)
- **Email Address:** ladyrk.kcrk@gmail.com
